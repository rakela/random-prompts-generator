## FAQ for Store Listing

**Q: Is this extension really free?**
A: Yes! Random Prompts Generator is completely free with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium features.

**Q: Does it work offline?**
A: Absolutely! The extension works perfectly without an internet connection.

**Q: Do you collect any data?**
A: No. We don't collect, track, or transmit any user data. Your privacy is guaranteed.

**Q: Can I use the prompts commercially?**
A: Yes! You own any prompts you generate and can use them for personal or commercial projects.

**Q: How do I save prompts?**
A: Click the "Save" button on any generated prompt. Saved prompts appear in the Saved Prompts section.

**Q: Can I customize prompt categories?**
A: Not in the current version, but we're considering this feature for future updates!

**Q: Will there be more categories?**
A: We're always working on improvements! Visit our website to suggest new categories.
